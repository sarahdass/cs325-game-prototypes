Archer vs Ghosts

  • Originality: The player can now move up and down. The goal is to get as many bullets as possible without touching the ghosts.
  • Technical Merit: The player can move up, down, left, and right.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to use sprites and how to move a body
  • Prototype Assets:
    • Archer: https://opengameart.org/content/animated-ranger-modification-original-calciumtrice
    • Electric Balls: https://opengameart.org/content/energy-ball
    • Ghosts: https://opengameart.org/content/ghost-animated
    • Explosion: https://opengameart.org/content/electrical-disintegration-animation
    • Arrow: https://opengameart.org/content/arrow-0

  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This was from a copy from the Phaser 2 examples
  • High Concept: Try to catch as many electric balls as possible without touching the ghosts.
  • Theme: It has a fantasy theme
  • Mandated Variety: This game is an arcade game, third person shooter.
  • Prototype Goal: It is evaluating movement of sprites and bodies.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience the thrill of fighting an army of ghosts with a bow and arrow.
  • Gameplay: Players can move their characters around using the arrows. To shoot, the player must press the space bar. The player can only shoot upward.
  • Strategies: Try to collect a lot of energy blasts and shoot the ghosts for even more points.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: This is a fight between the ghosts and the archer. The ghosts think their blasts hurt the archer, but it actually helps him. The archer, however, cannot handle the power of the ghosts when he touches them and dies.
  • Target Audience: This game is for everyone who enjoys arcade games.
  • Play Time: It can take from <1 min to 5 minutes
