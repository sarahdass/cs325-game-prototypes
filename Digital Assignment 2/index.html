Tiny Ghost Game

  • Originality: Bad guys were added and the map is new.
  • Technical Merit: Players can jump around and scare the guys.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to use tilemaps and phaser physics.
  • Prototype Assets: The tile map was made on mapediter
    • Guy: Guy
    • ghost: ghost
    • background: background
    • tileset: tileset
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This game was copied from the phaser example starstruck. Some code from invaders was also used.
  • High Concept: Players must scare away all the lost tourists.
  • Theme: There is a supernatural theme.
  • Mandated Variety: This is a platformer game.
  • Prototype Goal: The player will use the space bar to jump and the arrows to move back and forth.
  • Player Experience Goals: Players should experience the joy of scaring the guys.
  • Gameplay: Players can jump and move around. When they collide with a guy the guy will disappear. Once all the guys are gone, the player wins.
  • Strategies: Players should figure out the best platforms to use to get to the guy.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The players are a tiny ghost who is trying to protect their home from the tourists who are lost due to the poor calculations of their travel agent.
  • Target Audience: This game is for anyone who enjoys supernatural themes.
  • Play Time: It takes less that 3 minutes to play
