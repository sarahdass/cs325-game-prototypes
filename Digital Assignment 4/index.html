  • High Concept: Players must manipulate their surroundings to escape.
  • Theme: This is a puzzle plat-former game.
  • Mandated Variety: This is a plat-former game that uses the arrows to jump and move.
  • Prototype Goal: This is evaluating level change and how to make each level more challenging and build off what the gamer learned from the previous level.
  • Player Experience Goals: The player should experience the challenge of trying figure out the best way to escape.
  • Gameplay: Players will use the arrows to jump and move side to side. At level 1 players learn they can manipulate their environment to get out. Level 2, they realize that not all escape routes are as they seem.
  • Strategies: Players should use the boxes around them to jump on areas they cannot reach.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The player is a chicken who is trying to escape the farm after recently hearing that they are about to be dinner.
  • Target Audience: Anyone who likes trying to figure out how to get out places.
  • Play Time: It takes 1-3 minutes
